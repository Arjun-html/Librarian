About this Publication

The Arjun Dispatch is Arjun's reading journal, recording the books he reads on topics ranging from software, engineering, mathematics, finance, philosophy, geopolitics — alongside the notes and reflections each one leaves behind.

Think of it less as a catalogue and more as a desk: works currently under review lead the front page, while the full shelf lives in the Library. Longer reflections appear in Essays & Thoughts.

Arjun is a sophomore at the University of Hong Kong, majoring in Electronics Engineering. He is always looking to learn and explore further down the unbeaten path, and is always open to a chat.
