Notes on Reading Slowly

Why I stopped racing through books and started keeping a commonplace book instead.

For a long time I measured reading by velocity — pages per hour, books per month. The number went up; the retention did not.

The turn

What changed was keeping a commonplace book: a single place to copy out the lines worth keeping, in my own hand.

  • A sentence you copy is a sentence you actually read.
  • Re-reading your notes is cheaper than re-reading the book.
  • The act of choosing what to keep is the comprehension.

Slow reading isn't slower, in the end. It's the only reading that compounds.
