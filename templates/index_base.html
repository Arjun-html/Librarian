About this Publication

The Arjun Dispatch is the personal reading journal of Arjun, a university student of Electrical/Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science. It records the books he reads — software, engineering and mathematics, finance, and philosophy — alongside the notes and reflections each one leaves behind.

Think of it less as a catalogue and more as a desk: works currently under review lead the front page, while the full shelf lives in the Library. Longer reflections appear in Essays & Thoughts.
